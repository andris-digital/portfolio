Dorset Local Enterprise Partnership (DLEP)

The Dorset Local Enterprise Partnership is a business-led private and public sector partnership that aims to promote local economic growth and prosperity. Designed and printed using the litho print technique.

Bournemouth Natural Science Society (BNSS)

Floor plan and welcoming leaflet for Bournemouth Natural Science Society. The aim was to create a floor plan that was integrated into the welcoming brochure. It had to help visitors to navigate through the confusing layout of the museum rooms.

Bournemouth University Self Help Books

Two A4 64-page books and an A6 pocketbook. The student book helps students who have mental difficulties to organise their studies. The tutor's book helps employees to work with students who have learning difficulties. Later on, an additional supporting app was created. The client particularly wanted the books to be in a landscape format. The books follow the strict Bournemouth University brand guidelines and use a bright colour palette throughout.
