Dorset Cottage Holidays

Dorset Cottage Holidays is a family-owned business offering holiday cottage rentals across Dorset. Their previous website was outdated, slow, and not optimised for mobile devices, making it difficult for users to browse and book properties.

The project focused on migrating all existing content to WordPress, redesigning the layout, and creating a visually consistent, easy-to-use interface. I worked closely with a separate development team to design a custom search and booking plugin, and later took part in implementing and refining it so it could be easily managed by the business owner.
